**SLIDE 8: HOW IT WORKS — The MVP** *Three layers. One product.(Chris)*

**Say:** "Let me show you how it actually works under the hood.

Layer one is Pep the companion. Daily check-ins, emotional states, streaks. Think Duolingo — the more you check in, the more Pep thrives. The habit forms. The data gets richer.

Layer two is the signal engine. It runs what we call a divergence detection system. If someone is saying everything is fine but their behavioral data — meeting participation, response times, check-in rates — tells a different story, Pep flags it. Because masking is the number one reason distress goes undetected. We built the product to catch what people are hiding.

Layer three is the manager dashboard. Plain language. Actionable signals. Conversation prompts. No individual data exposed. No surveillance. Just early visibility.

We have a working MVP and we'll demo it right after this."
