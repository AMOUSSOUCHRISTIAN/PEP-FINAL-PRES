Employees stay silent — not from lack of trust, but from fear of being seen as weak. The detection gap is real, consistent, and expensive.

Why existing tools fail

Current tools either survey employees or surveil them.

Unmind · Leapsome
Built for the employee or HR. Rely on self-reporting — only the willing answer honestly.
Officevibe · 15Five
Pulse surveys & performance. Low adoption, lagging signal, easy to game.
Kona
Manager-adjacent, but no early visibility into how a team is actually doing.
Manager training
Better skills, zero data. You can't act on what you can't see.
None give managers early visibility without self-reporting or surveillance. That's the gap where Pep lives.

How it works — three layers, one product
Layer 01
The Companion
Lives in the corner of your screen. Daily check-ins, emotional states, streaks. It helps employees organise their day, decompress, prep a hard conversation.
Think Duolingo — habit compounds
Layer 02
The Signal Engine
Divergence detection. When someone says “fine” but behaviour says otherwise — participation, response times, check-in rates — Pep flags it. Built to catch what people mask.
Masking is the #1 reason distress hides
Layer 03
The Manager Dashboard
Plain-language, actionable signals and conversation prompts. Team-level patterns only — who went quiet, where check-ins dropped.
No individual data · No surveillance
